Destinations we love to stitch together

Every itinerary is a blend of icons and hidden corners — tell us your pace and we’ll balance both.

Srinagar

Dal & Nigeen lakes, old city walks, handicrafts, and the calm rhythm of life on the water.

Gulmarg

Gondola views, meadows, and winter sports when the season allows — year-round mountain drama.

Pahalgam & Aru

River picnics, Betaab Valley, and pine-scented drives — the softer side of the Pir Panjal.

Sonamarg

High-altitude day trips, alpine light, and the road opening towards Ladakh for longer adventures.

Jammu & Katra

Gateway to Vaishno Devi, palace architecture, and the warmer, boulder-strewn foothills.

Off-map gems

Yusmarg, Doodhpathri, Gurez — when you have time, we’ll open doors beyond the usual circuit.
